Nicholas Silva - About Me

Hi! I'm a junior English major at UMass Amherst, with a concntration in Creative Writing. I am also pursuing the PWTC certificate.

I have a great interest in writing, and have been writing creatively since the fourth grade! I'm very excited to be taking two code-related courses this semester, as I would love to write for games --my own or otherwise! In my free time, I enjoy writing, gaming, and reading. I also enjoy spending time outdoors, hiking and exploring local parks.
As all of this might suggest, I am a massive nerd. I really enjoy sci-fi and fantasy, and some of my favorite franchises include Star Wars, Star Trek, and Lord of the Rings. Some of my favorite games include Minecraft, Sims 4, and Jedi: Fallen Order. You can often find me browsing the shelves of the closest Barnes and Noble.

On a tangential note to such nerdy talk, if you're looking for something humorous to watch, I would highly recommend YouTuber Deep Blue Ink's animation of Dropout's Make Some Noise "Times New Roman vs. Comic Sans - Animated". It is easily one of my favorite videos, and I think that that probably speaks volumes about me.
